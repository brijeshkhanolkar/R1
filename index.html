The Real Problem

Why people die at road accidents

It's not lack of emergency services. It's human behavior driven by systemic failures.

⚖️

Bystander paralysis

In BIMSTEC countries, stopping to help can get you detained by police, dragged into court cases for months, and harassed as a suspect. Good Samaritan laws exist but nobody knows about them.

🚑

Slow routing to wrong responder

112 and 108 only connect to government ambulances. Private ambulances exist and are faster in cities but nobody calls them in emergencies. Victims wait 35 minutes when a private ambulance could reach in 8.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family in the dark for hours

The victim's family has no idea an accident happened until police call — sometimes 2-3 hours later. During that window, family could have reached the hospital and provided critical medical history.

📵

No internet at accident sites

Highways and rural roads in BIMSTEC have poor or no signal. Emergency apps that require internet fail exactly when you need them most.

The Solution

How GoodStop works

One tap. Everything handled. No registration. No legal risk.

01

Technology Stack

Built to ship

Every technology choice is justified. Every component is implementable in a hackathon timeline.

FRONTEND & OFFLINE
Progressive Web App (PWA)
Installable app with Service Workers for offline caching and zero-install deployment.
COMPUTER VISION
TensorFlow.js (MobileNet)
On-device AI image classification for accident severity. No cloud latency.
TELE-MEDICINE
WebRTC
Peer-to-peer live video and audio streaming for trauma center guidance.
MAPS & ROUTING
Leaflet + Overpass API
Live querying of OpenStreetMap for real nearby hospitals and accurate routing.
DYNAMIC ETA
Open-Meteo API
Live weather fetched via GPS to penalize ambulance ETA in rain, fog, or snow.
AI CHATBOT
OpenAI API
Multilingual guidance and law info. Context-aware dynamic responses.
REVERSE GEOCODING
Nominatim API
Converts GPS to country code to display the correct Good Samaritan Law instantly.
SMS FALLBACK
Native SMS URI
Hooks into mobile messaging app to dispatch GPS offline via 2G networks.
